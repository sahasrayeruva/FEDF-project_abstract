What We Offer

Every Tool to Run a Smarter Bar

From minibar logs to admin controls — purpose-built for hospitality efficiency.

📋
Minibar Log & Consumption

Track every item consumed per room with timestamps. Automated forms eliminate manual entry and billing discrepancies.

🔄
Replenishment Scheduler

Live workflow tracker lets housekeeping update room servicing in real time. Managers see pending, complete, and delayed rooms instantly.

📊
Inventory Charts & Analytics

Visual dashboards powered by Chart.js display stock levels, usage patterns, and inventory insights — all at a glance.

🔔
Low Stock Alerts

Automatic notifications trigger when items fall below threshold. Never let a room go understocked or a billing opportunity slip away.

🧾
Billing Reconciliation

Match consumption records with billing data seamlessly. Breakage records, corrections, and export-ready reports simplify accounting.

🛡️
Admin Panel & Authentication

JWT-secured login with role-based access. Full admin control over staff, inventory data, activity timeline, and configuration.

Process Flow

How SmartSense Works

Four seamless steps from guest consumption to settled invoice.

01
Guest Consumes

Items are taken from the minibar or hotel bar during the guest's stay.

02
Staff Logs Entry

Housekeeping logs consumption digitally — timestamped and staff-attributed.

03
Inventory Updates

Stock levels adjust automatically. Alerts and replenishment tasks trigger instantly.

04
Bill Reconciled

Charges sync to billing. Reports generated and exported for accounting in one click.
